Session 4 — The Descent Begins

The party finally arrived at the jagged entrance of the Whispering Caves as the blood-red sun dipped below the Horizon of Ash. The air grew heavy with the smell of wet stone and ancient decay. Thrain felt a distinct vibration in his warhammer, a warning from the ancestors that the ground here was not merely rock, but something more... visceral.

"Do not listen to the echoes of your own voice," the hermit had warned. "In the descent, every sound you make is a debt you'll eventually have to pay."

Key Objectives & Encounters

  • The Maw of Silence: A skill challenge (DC 14 Athletics/Acrobatics) to navigate the crumbling descent into the first chamber. Failure results in 2d6 bludgeoning damage and alerting the scouts.
  • The Pale-Eyed Sentinels: Three Deep-Ghouls are currently feasting on a carcass near the eastern tunnel. If the party uses Stealth (DC 12), they can bypass them.
  • The Rune-Coded Lock: A stone door with glowing purple glyphs. Requires the Crescent Key found in Session 2 or a DC 18 Arcana check to bypass.

As they stepped into the darkness, the ambient light of the surface faded instantly. It was as if the darkness itself was aggressive, pushing against their torches. This is where the real campaign begins.
